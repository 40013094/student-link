Photopea

Photopea is a in-browser image editor that supports many formats such as .PNG, .JPG, .sketch, .PSD, and .SVG, as well as a few others I haven't mentioned. It's comparable to other editors like Photoshop or GIMP, but it runs in the browser and is 100% free & open source. (This means that the source code is available to download and edit.)

Backchannel Chat

Backchannel Chat is a free backchannel chatroom similat to Today's Meet. It has custom icons, a profanity filter, and is awesome for talking with, say, a group outside of school.
